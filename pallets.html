Condition Grade Key

Customer Returns

Previously purchased items returned by customers. Packaging may be opened, and condition can range from like-new to incomplete.

Overstock

Excess inventory from retailers or distribution channels. Usually the cleanest grade and often the best fit for resale-ready programs.

Shelf-Pulls

Store-removed merchandise that may have packaging wear, stickers, or discontinued SKUs but was not necessarily sold to a consumer.

Salvage

Heavier risk inventory for experienced buyers. May include damaged, incomplete, or non-working items typically priced accordingly.

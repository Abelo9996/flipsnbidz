Our Policies

Please schedule your pickup before arrival!

All items must be picked up within 7 days of the auction closing or they will be forfeited. Pickups are available Wednesday through Monday from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Items not claimed within this time frame will be forfeited with no refunds. We will not hold any items beyond 7 days.

Buyers are responsible for the removal of purchased items. When requesting assistance with loading, you acknowledge that Flips and Bidz is not responsible for any injuries, damages, or accidents resulting from loading, securing, or transportation of purchased items during or after the pickup process.

We do not provide shipping.

Accepted payment methods are credit cards. Payments through cash apps are accepted upon approval and contact.

Fees:

  • 12.50% buyer's premium
  • 3% processing fee
  • 9.5% sales tax

All payments must be made in full before any items are removed. The card on file will be charged at the end of the auction. Please ensure you register with the card you intend to use. A $1 temporary charge will be applied during registration to verify your card, which will be refunded.

All items are sold "as is, where is," with no warranties or guarantees, express or implied, including but not limited to merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, or non-infringement.

Flips and Bidz makes no representations or warranties regarding the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of item descriptions, images, or condition reports.

We invite all bidders to visit Wednesday through Monday between 10:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. to inspect items in person before bidding. You may also contact us for additional condition information about specific lots before placing a bid.

Buyers assume all risks associated with the purchase, use, transport, and resale of items. Once an item is purchased, the buyer waives any claims against Flips and Bidz regarding its condition, usability, or value.

We are committed to accuracy in our listings and transparency in item conditions.

If an item is incorrectly listed, you are eligible for a full refund, provided you notify us within 7 days of pickup. If the item's condition differs from what is stated in the listing title or description and is not correctly represented in the provided images, we will issue a full refund upon return.
